As [Bison Wallet](https://github.com/decred/dcrdex) (aka dcrdex) recently
reached maturity with its 1.0 release, it is now considered in scope for the
bounty program.
The [atomicswap proof-of-concept](https://github.com/decred/atomicswap) repo
which preceded Bison Wallet has correspondingly been removed from scope.
